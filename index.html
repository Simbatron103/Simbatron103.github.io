Gremlin Relations

About the Gremlin Relations Department The Gremlin Relations Department is GremlingCove™’s elite (and slightly chaotic) customer service unit. Staffed entirely by highly trained, multilingual Gremlins in tiny suits, their mission is to handle disputes, complaints, and unexplained phenomena with a mix of diplomacy and spontaneous interpretive dance. Services include: Translating Gremlin growls into human language Delivering cryptic messages sealed in jam jars Please note: They do not respond to emails. You must summon them by placing three mismatched socks in a triangle and whispering, “I need a chat.” Response time: 4–7 business moons.
